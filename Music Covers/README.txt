These pieces were my first ever designs made in Adobe Illustrator. They were created for the first major grade in my Digital Graphics class: the Music Covers project. For this project, we were tasked with creating two designs for two different songs. Our goal was to give the viewer an idea of what the song/album would sound like based on the design while remaining abstract.

"&" by Tally Hall: For this work, I made reference to the theme of duality in *Good & Evil* with the split down the middle of the design seperating the album into two halves. On the left side, shapes are lined in a pattern, symbolizing order. On the right side, jagged lines dart around like lightning, symbolizing chaos. The designs also make reference to the song's instrumentals, with the pattern on the left representing the stable drumline and the jagged lines on the right representing the sharp electric guitar. Keeping true to *Good & Evil*'s album cover, the design is black and white.

"Dream Sweet in Sea Major" by Miracle Musical: For this design, the beginning of the song was the main inspiration for the visuals. With nothing but wind, vocals, and the occasional piano note, the opening paints a picture of a strangely calming vaccum of space. The remainder of the song has an upbeat and almost whimsical feel, as if it is a pleasant dream. This is incorporated in the sphere's pastel dreamy color pallete and the ghost-like shapes dotted within the sphere.
